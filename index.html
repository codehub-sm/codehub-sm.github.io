WHO WE ARE

 At POORVAM, we are passionate about empowering individuals with Communication Disorders to unlock their full potential. Our dedicated program caters to every aspect of their development - academic, physical, social, behavioral, and emotional - ensuring they have an equal chance to succeed and thrive in society.

We embrace the power of active learning, guided by evidence-based practices, and place great value on individual experiences and interests. By fostering a strong bond between our therapists, patients, and their caregivers, we create a supportive environment that nurtures growth.

We strongly believe that true progress is a collaborative effort. That's why we actively involve parents and caregivers in the therapy process, equipping them with knowledge and tools to become genuine partners in their loved one's journey towards success.

From the moment you walk through our doors, until the day you achieve your therapy goals, we are unwavering in our commitment to your success. At POORVAM, your achievements fuel our inspiration, and together, we'll celebrate every milestone reached.

TEAM POORVAM

Team Poorvam includes qualified, licensed and experienced speech therapists, occupational therapists, and special educators. Therapists collaborate with other, clients, and families to create motivating and purposeful opportunities to further develop confidence.

Apoorva Rai, MASLP
Clinical Director

Our team is led by Apoorva Rai, MASLP who is licensed by the Rehabilitation Council of India and Indian Speech and Hearing Association, and has been a therapist over 13 years. She has a wealth of paediatric experience working with children with Cochlear implant, ASD, learning disabilities, ADHD, developmental delays, and Articulation Disorders. Her work as a speech therapist is driven by her passion for success by combining sensory-based strategies, Play based therapy and behavioural techniques in order to obtain maximum results.
