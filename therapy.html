Center For Speech Therapy

POORVAM INTERVENTION PROGRAM FOCUS ON DEVELOPING THE FOLLOWING SKILLS

  • Attention
  • Play skills
  • Cognition
  • Communication
  • Socialization
  • Pre-Academic skills
  • Physical Motor skills
  • Sensory integration
  • Balance strength & coordination

Progress of therapy is continuous and anecdotal observations are periodically marked using various checklists. We use an array of technological advances and a full continuum of program options to create individualized instruction to support the pursuit of lifelong learning

FAMILY ORIENTED INTERVENTION PROGRAM

We focus on working closely with the family as it is essential for treatment success. Family members are encouraged to participate in sessions and meet with therapists to discuss strategies for home to further support development and growth. Parents/caregivers play a key role in all the programs and we provide hands-on training, enabling them to become a POORVAM in the intervention process.
