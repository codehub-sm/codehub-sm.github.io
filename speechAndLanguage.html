Center For Better Tomorrow

Evaluation and treatment for individuals with communication disorders including but not limited to,

RECEPTIVE AND EXPRESSIVE LANGUAGE

Early intervention for children (2yrs to 5yrs) with Autism, ADHD, Syndromes, Hearing Impairment, Cochlear implanted

AUDITORY/LANGUAGE PROCESSING DISORDERS

Language based reading & writing deficits- dysgraphia, dyslexia

SOCIAL COMMUNICATION DISORDERS

Difficulties in social interaction due to autism or social pragmatic disorder

ARTICULATION AND PHONOLOGY

 Sound production errors, cleft lip and palate, Lisping, clarity of speech

VOICE

Aphonia, vocal fold paralysis, Vocal misuse -polyps/nodules

MOTOR SPEECH

Dysarthria, Apraxia, Oro-motor weakness, Facial palsy

NON- VERBAL CONDITION

Provide Augmentative alternative communication therapy

COORDINATE WITH SCHOOL SYSTEMS

To provide learning and intervention programs for children with special needs.

 

ASSESSMENT

We assess clients who have suspected speech (i.e., articulation, voice and/or fluency concerns) and/or understanding and using language (i.e., difficulty or delay in using words to communicate, putting together words and sentences, asking/answering questions, following directions, telling a story, describing or explaining a task, and/or developing social skills).

THERAPY

We provide one-on-one therapeutic intervention and group interactive sessions for children and adults with communication disorders,. We partner with parents or caregiver to develop an individualized therapy plan with short and long-term goals addressing areas of need. Therapy activities will centre on functional and contextual activities to improve skills and facilitate generalization to home, work and community environments
