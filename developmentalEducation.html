Our specially trained special educators and educational psychologists strive hard to make learning a fun through specially tailored pre academic and academic program

AREAS ADDRESSED

For children who have difficulties to reach up academic skills to the level as normal peers, we provide maximum support and intervention in areas like

  • Attention
  • Cognition
  • Eye hand coordination
  • Fine motor skills
  • Reading
  • Writing
  • Numeracy
  • Behavioural aspect of the child

HOW WE WORK

An Individual Education program is developed for each child by a team of trained professionals that includes Speech therapists, occupational therapists and special education teachers, as well as parents, understanding the various needs of the child.

Cognitive and literacy training is provided in three levels,

  1. Developmental education
  2. Preschool training
  3. Remedial education
